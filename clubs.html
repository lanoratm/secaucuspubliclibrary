Several clubs and groups meet at the library to share common interests and knowledge. 
New members are always welcome!

Camera Club
6:30 - 8:00 pm on the fourth Wednesday of the month
All levels of photography skill are welcome to join the Camera Club.  This group's focus is to explore the world of photography.  Members share their own work, demonstrate old and new photographic equipment, critique members' work, study famous artists, discuss color and black & white techniques, view educational and entertaining movies, experiment with photo enhancement software, enter contests and display work as a group. 

Hudson County Genealogical Society
11:00 am on the second Saturday of the month
The goal of the Hudson County Genealogical Society is to promote the study of genealogy and history of Hudson County New Jersey, to collect, preserve and index this information in cooperation with local libraries and Internet resources, and encourage adherence to the highest standards of research and documentation. We will strive to offer educational programs such as lectures, seminars and workshops and promote the observance of Family History month.  This active and dynamic group meets monthly at the library providing timely lectures and resources for searching for local genealogy.
Visit their website for more information Hudson County Genealogical Society

Needleworkers Round Table
6:30 - 8:00 pm on the second Tuesday of the month
Anyone who works with a needle is welcome to join in on the discussion and friendship.  We welcome all needle art workers such as knitters, crocheters and quilters!  Members share information, skill, ideas and patterns.  Bring your unfinished work or start something new.
