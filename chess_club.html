Join the Chess Club

Chess is a great game for improving your thinking skills and creativity. 

Don't know how to play?  That's okay.  You will learn while having fun and challenging others. 

Chess Club runs from October to June at 3:30pm and is open to any child ages 5 and up. Children under 6 must have a parent present.
