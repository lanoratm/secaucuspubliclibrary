What is the Summer Reading Club?
The Summer Reading Club is a program that encourages summertime reading.  Every time your child visits the library and checks out books, he or she can enter our weekly drawing (one raffle ticket per day) for a gift card to Toys 'R Us or Kerasotes Movie Theater.  If your child completes our Summer Reading Club Challenge, he or she can join us for a Summer Reading Club Pizza Picnic, sponsored by Natoli's Pizza, in the Children's Reading Garden on Thursday, August 9 at 11:00 am. 

Who can join?
All Secaucus children ages 3 and up can participate.

How do I sign up?
To be part of the Summer Reading Club, just visit the library and fill out a short form.  This form will keep track of what you read throughout the summer.

What is the Summer Reading Club Challenge?
Children and teens who participate in the Summer Reading Club and who read a certain amount of books throughout the summer, will be invited to the Summer Reading Club Pizza Picnic, sponsored by Natoli's Pizza, in the Children's Reading Garden on Thursday, August 9 at 11:00 am.  (rain date is Friday, August 10 at 11:00 am). 

When does the Summer Reading Club begin?
The club runs from Friday, July 1 until Wednesday, August 8. 

When do the raffle tickets get pulled for the gift cards?
Raffle tickets are pulled every Monday morning from July 11 until August 8.

Gift cards are sponsored by the Friends of the Secaucus Library.
