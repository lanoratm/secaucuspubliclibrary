What if I do not return an item on time?
See the loan chart for loan periods and overdue fees. You will not be able to check out additional items if you have more than $10 in overdue fines on your account.  You will be sent overdue reminders by mail or by email if we have your email account on file.  After receiving three overdue notices you will be charged for the assumed-lost cost of the item. 

What if I lose an item?
You will be charged for the library items you lose.  Replacement costs depend on the item. 

To check the status of your account, click Log in to My Account located at the top of every page or call (201) 330-2083 during operating hours.  Have your library card with you.
