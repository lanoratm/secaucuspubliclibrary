Why should I have a library card?
A library card is very special.  It allows you to borrow books, movies, music, magazines and more for free.  With your card, you can also use library computers.  In addition, you can log on to databases for homework help at home with your library card.

Renew Borrowed Items
You can renew items from home with a password.  If you don't have a password, ask a children's librarian for one.

Check Your Card
See what items you checked out, have on hold, and when they are due.

What If I lose my card?
Having a library card is a big responsibility.  If you lose it, you will need to buy a new one for $3.00. 

Can I lend my card to a friend?
No, your library card is for you only.  If you lend it to a friend and they check out books and don't return them on time, then you have to pay the fine, not them.
