This Year's Goal
The proceeds raised from this year's Mini-Fair will benefit the expansion and upgrade of the Secaucus Public Library's technology equipment and services. Over the next year, we hope to upgrade the library's free WIFI system, increasing not only its bandwith, but also its range, so that all library meeting rooms and departments will have full-strength WIFI access.

Funds may also be used to upgrade existing library computers and technology-related equipment, or to provide technology-related classes and workshops for children and adults within our community.

What is the Mini-Fair?
The Mini-Fair is the Friends of the Library's largest fundraising and community event of the year. It has become a Secaucus tradition for people of all ages and includes a large used book sale, baking contest, flea-market and craft-fair style vendors, food vendors, live entertainment, free crafts and a pie-eating contest for children, chess competitions, games, face-painting, great raffle baskets, a fair-share raffle and much more! Each year, the fair draws thousands of people and raises money for a targeted project for the library.

Vendors
If you would like to be a vendor at the Mini-Fair, please download a vendor contract here and return it to Jenifer May at the Secaucus Public Library as soon as possible. You will receive a confirmation by phone or email when your application has been approved.

Table space costs $20 for a table up to 8 feet and $35 dollars for a 16 foot space.

Donate
There are many ways in which you can donate to the Mini-Fair.
Below are some of the ways that you can help us reach our goal:

  • Donate gently used books, CDs, and movies for our book sale
  • Donate items to be sold on our White Elephant table
  • Provide a raffle basket (value of $50.00 or more)
  • Monetary donations
For more information about donating to the Mini-Fair and Book Sale, please contact the library during operating hours or e-mail us.. To download a donation form, click here.

Fair Share Raffle
For a chance to win big, buy a fair share raffle and support the Friends at the same time! Tickets are $1.00 each and books of tickets are $10.00. Chances can be purchased at the library at any time and at the Mini-Fair. The raffle will be drawn at 3:00 pm on the day of the fair.

  • First prize is 50% of half of the total
  • Second prize is 30% of half of the total
  • Third prize is 20% of half of the total.
