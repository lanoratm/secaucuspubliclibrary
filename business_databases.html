Log on to these databases on any library computer or from home, using your library card. 
If you need assistance, please contact the reference department during operating hours.

Business & Company Resource Center (Infotrac)
"A comprehensive research tool that meets core curriculum requirements in undergraduate and graduate case study work as well as the business research needs of the job seeker or casual investor. It offers dynamic, accurate, and up-to-date topical business information and company and industry intelligence on thousands of global companies."

Business Module (Proquest)

Business Searching Interface (EBSCO)
"The Business Searching Interface provides an easy way to both browse and search for country economic data, company profiles, industry information and market research."

Business Source Corporate (EBSCO)
"Designed to meet the diverse information needs of today's companies. This database contains full text from nearly 3,000 quality magazines and journals, including sources ranging from general business periodicals such as Business Week, Forbes, Fortune and Fast Company, to trade publications such as Chemical Week, American Banker, and Drug Store News. The collection of titles also includes the top management journals such as Harvard Business Review, California Management Review and Academy of Management Perspectives.  Additional sources include more than 1,400 country economic reports from EIU, Global Insight, ICON Group International, CountryWatch, etc.; more than 10,150 substantial company profiles; and over 5,200 full-text industry reports."

Business Source Elite (EBSCO)
"Provides full text coverage for over 1,000 business periodicals ranging from general magazines to trade publications and top management journals."

Business Source Premier (EBSCO)
"This is the industry's most used business research database, providing the full text for more than 2,150 journals. Business Source Premier provides indexing, abstracts and full text back to 1886. Business Source Premier provides comprehensive coverage in all disciplines of business, including marketing, management, MIS, POM, accounting, banking, finance, etc. Additional full-text, non-journal content includes market research reports, industry reports, country reports, company profiles and SWOT analyses."

Career Insider
This database provides career assistance, 600+ career guides, and company information and profiles.  In addition, there are occupational profiles, message boards, and career advice articles.

Cypress Resume
This database provides resume and cover letter assistance.

General Business File ASAP (Infotrac)
"General Business File ASAP provides a combination of indexing, abstracts and full text for business and trade publications. Includes directory listings for companies as well as investment analysts' reports on major companies and industries."

Regional Business News (EBSCO)
"Provides comprehensive full text for regional US and Canadian business publications (including titles from Crain Communications)."

Reference USA
Reference USA is the leading provider of business and consumer research.  You can create marketing plans, conduct competitive analysis, and locate people.  The database contains more than 20 million businesses, 222 million consumers, over 680,000 healthcare providers, new U.S. businesses, and much more.
