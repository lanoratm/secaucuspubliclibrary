The library subscribes to several print newspapers and a large collection of electronic newspaper databases.  Print newspapers are kept for three weeks.  The databases can be accessed on any library computer or from home, using your library card.  If you need assistance, please contact the reference department during operating hours.
Newsbank
Access to nearly 1000 newspapers across the 50 states and several US Territories. Newsbank's introductory page gives links for direct access to the Jersey Journal (2003-present) and the Newark Star-Ledger (1989-present).

  • New York Times Magazine
  • The Record
  • The Star Ledger
  • The Times (London)
McClatchy-Tribune Collection (EBCSO)
Contains a 90-day archive of over 290 newspapers.

Newspaper Source (EBSCO)
"Cover-to-cover full text for 28 U.S. and international newspapers. The database also contains full text television & radio news transcripts, and selected full text for more than 260 U.S. newspapers."
